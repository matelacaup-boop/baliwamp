About Bangus Pond Monitor

System Overview

Bangus Fish Pond Water Quality Monitoring System is an innovative, solar-powered solution designed to help fish pond operators maintain optimal water conditions for healthy bangus production. By utilizing advanced IoT technology and real-time monitoring, we provide pond owners with the critical information they need to make informed decisions and prevent fish mortality due to poor water quality.

The system employs a buoy-based approach, deploying sensors directly in the pond to continuously measure five essential water quality parameters. All data is transmitted wirelessly to a cloud-based platform, accessible through user-friendly web interface from anywhere, at any time.

Water Quality Sensors

Dissolved Oxygen (DO)

Model: SEN0237

Measures oxygen levels essential for fish respiration and aquatic ecosystem health

Temperature

Model: DS18B20

Monitors water temperature to ensure optimal conditions for bangus growth

pH Level

Model: PH-4502C

Measures acidity/alkalinity to maintain ideal water chemistry for aquaculture

Salinity

Model: DFR0300

Tracks salt concentration in water, critical for brackish water pond management

Turbidity

Model: SEN0189

Measures water clarity and particle suspension to assess water quality
